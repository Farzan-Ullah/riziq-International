About Riziq International

Your Trusted Partner for Global Career Opportunities

Who We Are

Riziq International is a premier overseas job consultancy based in Pulwama, Kashmir. With years of experience in international recruitment, we have successfully placed hundreds of talented professionals in prestigious positions across Europe and Gulf countries.

Our team of dedicated experts understands the complexities of international employment and works tirelessly to ensure that every candidate receives personalized attention and support throughout their journey.

We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, transparency, and ethical practices in all our dealings.

"

Founder's Message

I started Riziq International with one purpose: to create a safe and transparent pathway for people seeking better opportunities abroad. As an Engineer, I witnessed firsthand how limited the job market is in regions like Kashmir, especially for engineering and business graduates. This pushed me to step into this field and use my experience of 5 years to guide others.

Riziq International is built on honesty, support, and genuine care. Every success story motivates me to keep expanding opportunities and helping candidates transform their lives.
