Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about overseas employment

How long does the job placement process take?
The timeline varies depending on the country and job type. Generally, the process takes 2-4 months from application to deployment. This includes job matching (1-2 weeks), interview process (2-4 weeks), offer letter (1 week), visa processing (4-8 weeks), and final deployment. We work to expedite the process while ensuring all documentation is properly completed.
What is your service fee structure?
Our fees vary based on the destination country and job type. We maintain complete transparency with no hidden charges. The fee typically covers CV preparation, job matching, interview coaching, documentation support, and visa assistance. We provide a detailed fee breakdown during your initial consultation. Payment plans are available for eligible candidates.
What documents are required for overseas job applications?
Basic documents include: Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity), Educational certificates and transcripts, Experience certificates and reference letters, Updated CV/Resume, Passport-size photographs, Medical fitness certificate, Police clearance certificate, and any professional licenses or certifications relevant to your field. Specific requirements may vary by country and job type.
How does the interview process work?
Most interviews are conducted via video call (Skype, Zoom, or WhatsApp). We provide comprehensive interview preparation including mock interviews, common questions, and tips. Some employers may conduct telephonic interviews first, followed by video interviews. In rare cases, in-person interviews may be arranged. We guide you through each step and provide feedback to improve your performance.
Do I need to know English or the local language?
English proficiency is required for most positions, especially in Gulf countries and English-speaking nations. The level required varies by job - basic English is sufficient for unskilled positions, while skilled roles may require fluent communication. For countries like Germany, Poland, or Romania, basic local language knowledge may be beneficial but not always mandatory. We can guide you on language requirements for specific positions.
What is the visa processing time?
Visa processing times vary by country: Gulf countries (UAE, Saudi, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman) typically take 3-6 weeks. European countries may take 6-12 weeks. The UK takes 4-8 weeks. Processing times can be affected by embassy workload, document completeness, and country-specific regulations. We track your application and keep you updated throughout the process.
Are the jobs you offer genuine and verified?
Absolutely! We work only with verified, reputable employers. All job offers are genuine with proper employment contracts. We conduct thorough verification of employers before partnering with them. You will receive an official offer letter with complete job details including salary, benefits, working hours, and contract duration. We never charge fees for fake or non-existent positions.
What are the eligibility criteria for overseas jobs?
General eligibility includes: Age between 21-45 years (varies by job), Valid passport, Good health (medical fitness required), No criminal record, Educational qualifications as per job requirements, and Relevant work experience (for skilled positions). Specific jobs may have additional requirements. We assess your profile during consultation and recommend suitable opportunities.
Will I get accommodation and food in the destination country?
This depends on the employer and job contract. Many employers, especially in Gulf countries, provide accommodation and sometimes food as part of the employment package. For European jobs, accommodation may or may not be provided - this is clearly mentioned in the offer letter. We ensure all benefits are documented in your contract before you accept the offer.
Can I apply for jobs in multiple countries?
Yes, you can apply for positions in multiple countries. However, we recommend focusing on 2-3 preferred destinations to increase your chances. During your profile assessment, we'll discuss which countries best match your qualifications, experience, and career goals. This targeted approach typically yields better results than applying everywhere.
What support do you provide after I get the job?
Our support continues even after placement! We assist with pre-departure orientation, travel arrangements guidance, airport procedures, and settling-in advice. We maintain contact to ensure you're adjusting well. If you face any issues with your employer, we're here to help mediate and find solutions. Our relationship doesn't end with placement - your success is our success.
Is there an age limit for overseas jobs?
Age requirements vary by country and job type. Generally, most countries prefer candidates between 21-45 years. Gulf countries are more flexible with age for experienced professionals. European countries may have stricter age limits for certain visa categories. Unskilled positions typically have an upper age limit of 40-45 years. We'll advise you on age-appropriate opportunities during consultation.
